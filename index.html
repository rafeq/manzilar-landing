MANZILAR helps customers discover nearby sellers and lets local operators manage products, orders, dispatch, and notifications.

Customers Browse by town, order easily, save delivery addresses.
Sellers Manage products, availability, orders, and business details.
Couriers Receive dispatch updates, pickup details, and delivery routes.
Owners Assign roles, review requests, and support the marketplace.

For local customers

Customers choose their town, discover local sellers, place orders, and follow updates from checkout to delivery in one flow.

For home businesses

Sellers can present products clearly, set availability, manage prices, update order status, and coordinate delivery handoff.

For secure access

SMS is used only for user-requested one-time account verification and login verification inside the mobile app.

How MANZILAR uses SMS

  • Only for one-time signup and login verification codes.
  • Only after the user enters their own phone number and requests the code.
  • No promotional campaigns, no bulk messaging, and no purchased lists.
  • Initial rollout is limited to Israel.

Current product scope

  • Town-based marketplace browsing
  • Customer, seller, courier, and owner roles
  • Order tracking and dispatch handoff
  • Invitation and role-management workflows
